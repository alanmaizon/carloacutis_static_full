Relic Visit Programme

The relic of Blessed Carlo Acutis is available for visits to parishes, schools, and diocesan events. The programme fosters devotion to the Eucharist and promotes Carlo’s message of holiness in everyday life.

Relic visits typically include:

  • Mass and exposition of the relic
  • Talks about Carlo’s life
  • Veneration time and prayer
  • Multimedia exhibition (optional)
