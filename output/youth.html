Youth Involvement

Carlo Acutis continues to inspire young people across the world. Through his example, youth are encouraged to explore their faith in a joyful and authentic way.

Our youth programmes aim to engage students and young adults in retreats, workshops, and online outreach rooted in the Eucharistic devotion that defined Carlo’s life.

  • Retreats and prayer groups
  • Online content created by youth
  • Local parish involvement
  • Testimonies from students inspired by Carlo
